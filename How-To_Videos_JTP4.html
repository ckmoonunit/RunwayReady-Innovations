Oshkosh Jetpower 4 Field Test

The video and written instructions below demonstrates how to field test a JTP 4 unit that's triggering an IGBT Fault.


  • Preparation: Power down the frequency converter and disconnect the input voltage source. Connect IGBT Functionality Tester to a 120VAC source.
  • Disconnect feedback bus wires: Disconnect the negative DC bus wire 315 at both locations as well as removing the IGBT drive board for easier access. Click here to view the bus wire layout.
    ×
    Next, remove the appropriate gate connections on the IGBT
  • Connect the IGBT Functionality Tester: Connect IGBT functionality tester and the tester button.by following the printed diagram on the tester.
