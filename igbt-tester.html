Welcome to the Future of Power Unit Diagnostics

Our innovative IGBT Functionality Tester is designed to streamline diagnostics, ensuring that power unit issues are identified swiftly and accurately. With the ability to test IGBTs directly within the unit, technicians save valuable time by avoiding unnecessary removals and quickly pinpointing the root of the issue. Not only does our tester confirm faulty components, but it also provides peace of mind by verifying that replacement IGBTs are fully operational, enhancing reliability and minimizing downtime.
